Privacy Policy - Just JSON

Just JSON does not collect or transmit any personal data.

All JSON formatting and comparison happen entirely in your browser and are not shared with any server.

For your convenience and to provide persistence across sessions, this extension stores your last formatted JSON input and your preferred theme (light/dark mode) locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API. This data is only accessible by the extension itself and remains entirely within your browser.

We do not use analytics, cookies, or any third-party services.

This extension is designed to work completely offline and prioritizes your privacy.

By using Just JSON, you agree to these terms.
